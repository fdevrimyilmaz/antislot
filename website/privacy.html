Privacy Policy

This policy explains data collection, retention, deletion, and support contact for Antislot.

What We Store

Antislot keeps core app preferences and recovery progress data needed to provide the app experience.

Retention

Telemetry and diagnostics retention is limited and controlled by in-app privacy settings. Default retention guidance is 30 days for buffered telemetry data.

Deletion

You can request data deletion support at any time by contacting support@antislot.app.

Data deletion requests are processed according to account and legal requirements.
